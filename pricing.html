Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free tier? +
The Free plan includes a verified agent profile, up to 3 property listings, basic analytics, and a lead capture form — no credit card required, no time limit. It's a limited plan designed for agents getting started. To add more listings, unlock advanced analytics, or remove SellingDubai branding, upgrade to Pro or Premium.
Can I switch plans anytime? +
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.
Is there a contract? +
No contracts at all. We operate on a month-to-month basis, and you can cancel your subscription anytime with no penalties. Your data remains yours.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Apple Pay through Stripe, ensuring secure and convenient payments for all our users worldwide.
What happens when I downgrade? +
You keep all your data when downgrading. Feature access adjusts at your next billing cycle. We'll help you export any data if needed during the transition.
